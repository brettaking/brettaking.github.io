Get Involved

  • So where does one start “getting involved”? Start by thinking of an issue that is important to you? What is an injustice you see in your community, in our society? What’s an issue that impacts you or your family? Then consider becoming an activist or volunteering your time with a group/organization that supports an issue that is important to you. Our Revolution works on these issues: www.ourrevolution.com/issues
